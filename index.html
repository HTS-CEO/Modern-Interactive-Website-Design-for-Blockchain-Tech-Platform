How It Works

Immutag links a tamper-proof tag on your device with a unique digital identity stored on the blockchain. Once tagged, your item becomes verifiable, transferable, and harder to steal or resell.

Tag your device

Apply the Immutag NFC tag to your valuable item for permanent identification.

Register it securely

Link your tag to a blockchain-verified digital identity through our secure app.

Prove ownership

Verify, transfer, or recover ownership anytime with immutable blockchain records.
