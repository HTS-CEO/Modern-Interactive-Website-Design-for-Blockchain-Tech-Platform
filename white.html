Technical Overview

Immutag leverages blockchain technology to create an unforgeable record of ownership. Our system combines tamper-proof NFC tags with smart contracts to establish a permanent link between physical items and their digital identities.

The decentralized nature of our solution ensures that ownership records remain secure, transparent, and accessible worldwide without relying on any single authority.

Token Economics

The Immutag ecosystem is powered by the TAGX/IMTG token, which serves multiple functions:

  • Payment for tag registration and ownership transfers
  • Staking for network security and governance
  • Rewards for validators and ecosystem participants
  • Access to premium features and services

Token distribution follows a carefully designed model to ensure sustainable growth and fair participation.

Use Cases

Immutag technology applies to numerous scenarios where proof of ownership is critical:

  • High-value electronics authentication
  • Luxury goods verification
  • Collectibles and art provenance
  • Insurance claim validation
  • Second-hand market transactions
  • Law enforcement asset tracking
